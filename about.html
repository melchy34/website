HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

De-Vessels School is an off-shoot of De-Vessels Nursery/Primary school which was born in September 2011. It was registered as a corporate body with registration number LAZ 1012010 on the 3rd of February 2025

Who We Are

To develop and produce successful scholars by offering qualitative education through the use of best qualified teachers with state of art/learning facilities.
