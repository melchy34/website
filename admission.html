Admission Process

Welcome to De-Vessels Schools! We are excited to have your child join our learning community. Our admission process is straightforward and designed to ensure that every student fits our educational environment.

Step 1: Application

Start by completing the application form below. Make sure to fill out all required fields accurately.

Step 2: Review

Once we receive your application, our team will review the details and schedule an interview if needed.

Step 3: Confirmation

If your application is accepted, you will receive an official confirmation and further instructions on completing your child's admission.

Apply Now

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of previous school records (if applicable)
  • Recent passport-sized photograph of the student
  • Payment of the application fee (if applicable)
